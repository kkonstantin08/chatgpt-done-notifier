ChatGPT Done Notifier

Conservative completion detection for ChatGPT, with desktop notifications and optional sound.

Behavior

Quiet Hours

Quiet hours suppress both desktop notifications and sound, including the built-in test actions.

Sound

The default sound is bundled with the extension. You can replace it with your own short audio file stored locally in the extension settings.
